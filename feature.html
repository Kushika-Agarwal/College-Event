Best Out of Waste

About the Event

The "Best Out of Waste" competition is an exciting and creative event where participants will transform waste materials into innovative and useful items. This event encourages students to think creatively, practice sustainability, and showcase their artistic skills by repurposing discarded materials into something valuable.

Guidelines

  • Eligibility: Open to all students of AMI
  • Team Composition: minimum 1 & maximum 5.
  • Duration: 30 minutes

Submission Deadline

  • Starting Date: 25 march
  • Last Date: 6 April

Results and Prize Claim

  • Result Announcement Results will be announced on same day.

Certificate policy

  • Certificates will be awarded to winners only.

Important Contacts

  • Email:mba.utkarsh@allenhouse.ac.in , bba.shweta@allenhouse.ac.in, bba.arun@allenhouse.ac.in
  • Mobile Phone:9889396935,8808986891,9838472944
  • Organizers: Mr. Utkarsh Singh Parihar, Ms. Shweta Chauhan, Mr. Arun Gupta

Rules

  • Task Overview: Participants must use waste or recyclable materials to create a unique and functional product.
  • Items such as newspapers, plastic bottles, old CDs, cardboard, fabric scraps, and other waste materials can be used.
  • Submission Format: No specific submission format is required.
  • Presentation: Participants must adhere to the time limit and maintain respectful discourse throughout the event.

Grading Criteria

Functionality and Performance: Judging will be based on creativity, usefulness, presentation, and the amount of waste reused.

Prize Distribution

  • Ist Place- Gold Medal and Certificate
  • IInd Place-Silver Medal and Certificate
  • IIIrd Place- Bronze Medal and Certificate

Judgement and Decisions

  • Judges’s Authority: The judges have the final authority regarding scoring and decisions.
  • Final Decisions:Decisions made by the judges are considered final.
