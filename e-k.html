About the Event

“EcoWकृति” is a celebration of sustainability, art, and innovation. This event aims to promote environmental consciousness through artistic expression, encouraging participants to use eco-friendly materials to create impactful artworks. It provides a platform for students and artists to explore the importance of recycling, sustainable living, and conservation through their creative vision.

The event also seeks to foster a culture of responsible creativity, where art is not only a form of expression but also a powerful medium to spread awareness about pressing environmental issues. Participants will have the opportunity to experiment with biodegradable, upcycled, and natural materials to produce artworks that inspire change and promote green practices.

Who Can Apply?

  • Open to students, artists, and creative individuals who have a passion for sustainability and art.
  • Participants can register individually or in teams of 2-4 members.
  • No prior experience in art is required—only
  • creativity, innovation, and an eco-conscious mindset.

Event Details

  • Event Date: (11.04.2025)
  • Event Duration: (10.30 AM- 03.00 PM)
  • Venue: Allenhouse Institute of Technology
  • Reporting Time: Participants must arrive at the venue at least 1 hour before the event begins.
  • Registration Deadline: (10.04.2025)
  • Result Announcement: (11.04.2025)

Process:

  • Registration: Participants must complete the registration form and pay the entry fee.
  • Theme Announcement: A unique theme focused on sustainability and environmental consciousness will be revealed before the event.
  • Artwork Creation: Participants will create art using recycled, biodegradable, or eco-friendly materials.
  • Participants will be provided with faulty or inefficient code and must debug & optimize it.
  • Submission & Display: Completed artworks will be displayed at an exhibition for evaluation and appreciation.
  • Judging & Evaluation: A panel of judges will assess the artworks based on creativity, eco-friendliness, theme relevance, and execution.
  • Awards & Recognition: The best artworks will be awarded certificates, medals, and cash prizes.
  • The fastest correct submission wins.

Rules & Regulations:

  • Eco-Friendly Materials: Participants must use recycled, biodegradable, or upcycled materials.
  • Originality: The artwork must be an original creation of the participant(s).
  • Size Limitations: Artworks should not exceed the given dimensions.
  • Time Limit: If it is a live event, participants will have a fixed time to complete their artwork.
  • Team Composition: Participants can register individually or in teams of 2-4 members.
  • Ethical & Thematic Guidelines: No offensive, political, or controversial content is allowed..
  • Venue & Workspace Etiquette: Participants must ensure cleanliness and dispose of waste responsibly.
  • Materials & Equipment: Participants should bring their own materials.
  • Judges' Decision: The judges’ decision will be final and binding.

Judging Criteria:

  • Creativity & Innovation (20%)
  • Eco-Friendliness (20%)
  • Theme Relevance (20%)
  • Aesthetic Appeal (20%)
  • Presentation & Execution (20%)

Prize Distribution:

  • 1st Place: Rs.1000 + Gold Medal & Certificate
  • 2nd Place:Rs.700 + Silver Medal & Certificate
  • 3rd Place: Rs.500 + Bronze Medal & Certificate )

Guidelines:

  • Participants must carry their college identity cards for verification.
  • Participants should arrange their own materials while adhering to event sustainability guidelines.
  • Any misconduct, rule violations, or use of prohibited materials will lead to immediate disqualification.
  • Final results will be announced on the concluding day of the event.
