About the Event

An exciting event designed for AI and IT enthusiasts! This challenge will test your problem-solving skills, and technical knowledge in the fields of Artificial Intelligence and Information Technology.

Guidelines

  • Eligibility:Open to all students of Allenhouse Business School.
  • Team Composition: Each participant competes individually.
  • Duration:90 min.There will be 50 MCQ Questions, Divided in 5 Rounds. (10 questions each round)

Rules

  • Task Overview:  There will be 50 MCQ Questions, Divided in 5 Rounds. (10 questions each round). Participants have to answer mcq questions by google form link.
  • Submission Format:  Google form link
  • Presentation:  Question will be asked by a power point presentation
  • Grading Criteria:   Each question carries 1 mark for right answer and 0 for wrong answer.
  • Functionality and Performance:   Evaluation will be considered for the fastest answers with in a proper time limit.
  • Event date: 12th April 2025

Prize Distribution

  • Ist Place- Gold Medal and Certificate
  • IInd Place-Silver Medal and Certificate
  • IIIrd Place- Bronze Medal and Certificate
