Business Plan Competition

About the Event

The Intercollege Business Plan Competition invites students to present innovative and viable business models. It provides a platform for aspiring entrepreneurs to showcase their ideas, receive expert feedback, and explore potential opportunities. The competition fosters creativity, strategic thinking, and practical business solutions, preparing participants for real-world entrepreneurial challenges.


Team Size

  • 2 Members

Important Dates & Deadlines:

  • Duration: 5 Hours
  • Date: 12th April 2025

Rules:

  • The competition comprises three stages: (i) Registration & Payment, (ii) Submission of Presentation, (iii) Delivery of Presentation.
  • Each team may have a maximum of two members only.
  • Submission Requirements:
    Presentations must be submitted in both PDF and Video formats.
    - Videos should not exceed 5 minutes and must feature AI-generated voiceovers.
  • Presentation Specifications:-
    Maximum of 20 slides (in PDF and video formats).
    - Presentations should address the following key areas:
    - Ideation and Environment Scan: (Need fulfillment, available alternatives, customer acceptance)
    - Feasibility Study: (Competition, price band, potential)
    - Market Offering: (Competitive strategies, differentiating factors)
    - Financial Projections: (Revenue model, forecasting)
    - Marketing Plan: (4Ps, SWOT analysis, etc.)
    - Model Timeline: (2–3 years)
  • Submission Deadline:
    - Email your presentation to mba.prashant@allenhouse.ac.in by 11:59 PM, 7th April 2025, using the subject line: Intercollege Business Competition_TeamName. - If already submitted, no further action is needed.
  • Presentation Duration: - Each team will have 20 minutes (15 minutes for presenting and 5 minutes for Q&A).

Prize Distribution-

  • Ist Place- Gold Medal and Certificate
  • IInd Place -Silver Medal and Certificate
  • IIIrd Place - Bronze Medal and Certificate
